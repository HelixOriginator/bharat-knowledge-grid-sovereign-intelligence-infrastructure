The Problem Space

Why India Needs a Knowledge Layer

Institutional Gatekeeping

Ideas and research remain trapped within closed institutional silos. BKG creates a transparent, permissionless pipeline where merit dictates visibility, not hierarchy.

Invisible Talent

Millions of skilled professionals and independent researchers operate without formal recognition. BKG maps capability to contribution, turning latent expertise into national capital.

Fragmented IP & Research

Patents, papers, and prototypes are scattered across disconnected platforms. BKG unifies intellectual assets into a verifiable, interoperable national ledger.

System Architecture

Four Pillars of Sovereign Intelligence

Designed for resilience, auditability, and civic-scale impact.

Sovereign Proof of Thought

Cryptographic timestamping establishes irrefutable Proof of Origin (POI) for ideas, research, and frameworks. Ensures intellectual ownership remains with the creator, not intermediaries.

Dharma Engine

An ethical evaluation layer that scores innovation against long-term civilizational impact, sustainability, and public good. Aligns progress with enduring societal values.

Local Intelligence Stack

Verifiable digital identity for professionals and creators. Integrates compliance, skill validation, and regional data sovereignty into a unified trust framework.

National Innovation Exchange

A structured marketplace connecting validated research with government initiatives and private startups. Accelerates translation from concept to deployment at scale.

Reputation as Currency

Credibility Scoring

Quantified, transparent metrics based on peer validation, implementation success, and ethical alignment. Not influenced by marketing or capital.

Capital Allocation

High-reputation contributors gain prioritized access to grants, institutional partnerships, and policy advisory roles. Trust replaces traditional collateral.

Beyond Tokens

Unlike speculative cryptocurrencies, BKG's reputation is non-transferable, effort-anchored, and aligned with measurable public contribution.

Strategic Alignment

The Next Layer of Digital India

India has successfully digitized identity and payments. The natural progression is digitizing and structuring knowledge itself. BKG operates as the intelligence mesh that sits atop existing infrastructure, ensuring that intellectual capital flows as efficiently as money and data.
