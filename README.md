🇮🇳 Bharat Knowledge Grid (BKG)
Sovereign Intelligence Infrastructure for India
Bharat Knowledge Grid (BKG) is a conceptual digital public infrastructure designed to transform how knowledge, innovation, and intellectual contributions are created, verified, and utilized in India.
It aims to make knowledge a first-class economic asset, similar to identity and payments.

🚀 Vision
India has built world-class infrastructure like Aadhaar (identity) and UPI (payments).
BKG is the next layer: knowledge.

A real-time, self-evolving knowledge economy for a self-reliant nation.


❗ Problem Statement


Independent researchers lack formal recognition


Institutional gatekeeping slows innovation


MSMEs and local professionals lack digital credibility


Intellectual contributions remain fragmented and underutilized


Policy and tech decisions often ignore long-term societal impact



💡 Core Concept
BKG is a sovereign, digital infrastructure layer that enables:


Verifiable ownership of ideas


Ethical evaluation of innovation


Discoverability of grassroots talent


Direct collaboration between creators and institutions



🧠 Architecture Overview
1. Sovereign Proof of Thought (SPoT)


Assigns Proof of Origin (POI) to ideas and research


Timestamped, verifiable intellectual ownership


Enables independent creators to participate without institutional barriers



2. Dharma Engine


Evaluates innovation based on long-term societal impact


Introduces a civilizational scoring framework


Moves beyond short-term ROI thinking



3. Local Intelligence Stack


Digital identity for professionals and MSMEs


Verifiable credentials and reputation


Simplified compliance and IP workflows



4. National Innovation Exchange


Marketplace for ideas and research


Connects:


Government


Startups


Independent creators




Enables funding, pilots, and deployment



🌐 Key Innovation: Reputation as Currency
BKG introduces a credibility-based system where:


Contributions are scored based on originality and impact


Reputation influences:


Grants


Hiring


Policy participation




This creates a trust-driven knowledge economy, not just a financial one.

🇮🇳 Why It Matters


Supports Atmanirbhar Bharat (self-reliance)


Unlocks India’s hidden intellectual capital


Reduces dependence on institutional gatekeeping


Enables scalable, transparent innovation
